About SnakeGuard Plus

SnakeGuard Plus is a life-saving initiative developed to help people in snake-prone areas identify snakes and react quickly and correctly to snake encounters and bites.

Our platform uses machine learning to analyze images of snakes or snake bites and provide users with quick identification, helping them access correct treatment information and nearby hospitals instantly.

We are passionate about spreading awareness, debunking dangerous myths, and offering a centralized place for users to request help and learn about different snake species.

Developed by a dedicated team of final-year Computer Science students, our mission is to reduce snakebite-related fatalities through technology, education, and fast response.
