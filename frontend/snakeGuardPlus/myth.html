Snake Myths and Facts

Myth 1: All snakes are venomous
Only a small percentage of snakes are actually venomous. Most are harmless and help control pests.
Myth 2: Snakes chase humans
Snakes do not chase humans. They might seem to follow you when they are trying to escape in the same direction.
Myth 3: Baby snakes are more dangerous than adults
While it's true they may inject all their venom, their venom yield is lower than that of adults.
Myth 4: You should suck the venom out of a bite
This is dangerous and ineffective. Seek immediate medical attention instead.
Myth 5: Cutting the bite area helps
Cutting the wound can worsen the injury and lead to infection.
